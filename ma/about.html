From Player to Creator

Bonjour! I'm Dino Crafting, a Moroccan Minecraft content creator passionate about modding and community building. What started as a hobby playing Minecraft quickly turned into a mission to bring high-quality Minecraft content to Arabic-speaking players, especially my fellow Moroccans.

I believe gaming should be accessible to everyone, regardless of language. That's why I create content in Darija, English, and French — to reach as many players as possible. My mods are designed to enhance gameplay, fix issues, and add exciting new features to the game we all love.

When I'm not coding mods or recording videos, you'll find me on the Discord server chatting with the community, brainstorming new ideas, or planning our next big project. Join the empire — let's build something amazing together!

Modding Philosophy

Every mod I create aims to solve real problems players face, whether it's inventory management, performance issues, or quality-of-life improvements. I prioritize stability, compatibility, and user experience. All mods are free, open-source, and built with love for the Minecraft community.
